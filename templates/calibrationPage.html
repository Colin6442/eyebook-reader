In order for the eye tracking to work it must first be calibrated:

Make sure you are in a well lit environment. Once calibration starts you will need to look at the red circle and click on it. There will be a total of ~20 calibration circles you will need to click while looking at them.

We have detected that you have completed calibration before. Please click the "Check" button to see if the eye tracking is still suitable. If so then click continue, if not then click the "Reset" button to redo the calibration.
